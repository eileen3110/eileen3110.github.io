I fell in love with design at Tongji University, Shanghai, where I pursued Bachelor of Engineering in Landscape Architecture. I then went on to get a Master of Architecture from Pratt Institute, from where I have started working towards architecture licensure in NYC.
I am a highly responsible team player, and also have much experience leading and coordinating teams.

Forte

On top of proficient CAD skills and an obsession with refined and detailed modeling in Rhino and Maya, I am comfortable in producing quality presentations with visuals, audios, and motion pictures, constantly diving deeper into more tools that aid design and production (Grasshopper, Dynamo, AR/VR, Adobe Suite).

I enjoy yoga and bouldering, indulge in playing classical piano, and is always down for a game of chess or table tennis, or a night of social dancing!
